About Us

High Quality Pizza

Our Story

Welcome to our pizza restaurant in Ermones, Corfu! We are passionate about serving delicious, authentic pizza to our customers, and we are excited to share our story with you.

At our restaurant, we take pride in using only the highest quality ingredients. We source our flour from local mills, and our tomatoes are imported from the fertile regions of Italy. Our toppings are fresh and locally sourced, and we believe that our commitment to quality shines through in every bite.

Our Goals

Our commitment to excellence doesn't stop at the food. We believe that every customer who walks through our doors deserves the best possible dining experience. Our friendly staff is dedicated to providing outstanding service, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel at home.

Thank you for considering our pizza restaurant for your next meal. We hope to see you soon!
